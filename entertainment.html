Entertainment in Taniti

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do!

There is a local history museum, chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, and zip-lining in the rainforest.

For a local taste you can try visiting one of our pubs, or microbreweries. You could also try dancing at a new dance club, seeing a movie, taking helicopter rides, playing at an arcade, visiting art galleries, and bowling.

A nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year. Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
