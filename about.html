About Taniti

While Taniti is less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests

Taniti has a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. The local Tanitian indigenous population is about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
